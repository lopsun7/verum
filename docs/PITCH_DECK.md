# Aegis AI Hackathon Pitch Deck

## Slide 1. Aegis AI

Autonomous AI Treasury Infrastructure  
Predictive capital allocation across DeFi lending markets, chains, and stable assets.

## Slide 2. The Problem

- Treasury teams still chase current APY instead of forecasting future yield.
- Capital is fragmented across chains, protocols, wallets, and execution paths.
- Risk changes faster than human operators can react.
- Protocol APIs fail, gas conditions spike, and exploit risk can turn toxic in minutes.

## Slide 3. The Solution

Aegis AI is an autonomous treasury operating system that:

- predicts future APYs instead of reacting to present yields
- scores protocol and stablecoin risk in real time
- reallocates across chains before utilization shifts occur
- explains every move with expected gain, confidence, and detected risk

## Slide 4. Product Demo

- MetaMask-inspired wallet and treasury dashboard
- Bloomberg-like risk and performance terminal
- AI operations console with live agent logs
- autonomous reallocation engine with confidence scoring

## Slide 5. AI Architecture

- Yield Prediction Agent: LSTM, XGBoost, transformer forecast ensemble
- Risk Analysis Agent: exploit probability, oracle risk, governance concentration
- Sentiment Agent: governance forums, X/Twitter, protocol announcements
- Execution Agent: swaps, bridges, batching, MEV-aware routing
- Explainability Agent: human-readable rationale for every move

## Slide 6. Why We Win

- Predictive, not reactive
- Cross-chain and cross-protocol by design
- Institutional-grade explainability and defense mode
- Browser automation fallback using Actionbook when APIs fail
- Live DeFi intelligence via Bright Data

## Slide 7. Hackathon Stack

- AgentField for orchestration and shared memory
- Actionbook for fallback execution
- Bright Data for real-time protocol and sentiment scraping
- Qwen Cloud for reasoning-heavy risk analysis
- Qoder for Repo Wiki and AI engineering workflows
- Zeabur for deployment and CI/CD
- Z.ai / GLM for multimodal reporting and storytelling

## Slide 8. Business Value

- Higher net treasury yield after gas and risk adjustment
- Faster defense against protocol instability and depeg events
- Lower operational load for treasury teams
- Better auditability for fintech and institutional operators

## Slide 9. Roadmap

- Live protocol adapters and account abstraction signing
- policy-controlled autonomous execution approvals
- DAO treasury and fintech treasury onboarding
- insurance-aware routing and hedging overlays

## Slide 10. Vision

Aegis AI becomes the autonomous treasury layer for onchain capital:

- safe enough for institutions
- fast enough for crypto
- explainable enough for compliance
- intelligent enough to move before the market does
